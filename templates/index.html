Welcome

This site provides win rate, play rate and other general performance statistics for each of the civilisations in Age of Empires 2 DE. The statistics are generated for multiple different cohorts of matches where each cohort is defined by specific inclusion/exclusion criteria. Please click the "Cohorts" button on the top right of the page to get the full list of available cohorts.

Credit to aoe2.net for providing access to the data used to generate these statistics.
